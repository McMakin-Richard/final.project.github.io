Welcome To the 41st Century!

The Warhammer universe takes place in the far future of the 41st century. It is a time of brutal, endless conflict—all that remains is war. Humanity has evolved and spread across the stars, colonizing countless worlds for the imperium of man, the single largest and most powerful empire in human history. Yet despite the vast expanse and might of the empire, the imperium is beset upon all sides by dangerous and multitudinous threats that jeopardize humanities very existence. These threats can be categorized generally into two types: that of the forces of Chaos and the threat from the alien tides of the Xenos armies.

Here you will be able to explore the lore and history for each army/faction in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. Feel free to dive into the vast and detailed world of Warhammer and put yourself directly into the story to realize where you fit! If you already know which army interests you read on to explore.
