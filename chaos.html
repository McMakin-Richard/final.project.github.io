The Forces of Chaos

The Heretic Astartes…once the chaos space marines were ardent defenders of the Imperium. Now, these traitor legions seek to destroy the empire they once protected in service of Ancient, Malefic deities—the chaos gods Slaanesh, Khourne, Tzeentch, and Nurgle. Having abandoned their oaths of fealty to the Golden Throne, the followers of Chaos now live only to do their dark gods bidding and to see the galaxy burn.

Besides the heretic Astartes, the followers of chaos are myriad in both number and form. Numberless hordes of crazed cultists, grotesque mutants, and deranged daemons all owe allegiance to the gods of chaos and obey their orders eagerly.
